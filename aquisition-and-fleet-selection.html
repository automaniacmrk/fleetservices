Aquisition and Fleet Selection

It can be difficult to choose the best vehicles for your fleet. There are hundreds of makes and models to choose from, and the manufacturers always have nice things to say about a vehicle. A fleet service expert like Go Oil can help you choose the right vehicle for your business.

They know the difference between a loud engine and hauling power. They can identify the kind of seat needed for all-day drivers versus special assignment drivers. You can tell which vehicles get better mileage in urban areas versus those that are used for rural purposes.
