Fleet Analysis And Optimization

You can optimize your fleet's performance with fleet services. There is always improvement in business. With vehicles, this can often be seen as schedule tightness or driver satisfaction.

Different vehicles, or a different choice in maintenance, might provide you with better gas mileage per vehicle. You might also find vehicles with better driver technology, which could simplify your road-trip. You might also have cars that are past their prime years.

Our fleet services can assist you in identifying areas where efficiency is low or streamline your fleet management so you get the results you want. Fleet services can help you analyze and improve your fleet, whether it's a new leased vehicle or an upgrade to the driver technology. Once a plan is set out, your fleet services team can take care of or help you implement the optimizing changes.

Fuel Card Program

Want to implement fuel cards for your team? Fleet services can do that for you as well. Any time you have vehicles on the road, it's important that drivers not be asked to cover company gas out of pocket. However, it is important not to offer drivers a "blank check" that could be used for everything from gas fraud to road snacks.

Fuel cards are the solution to both problems because a well-designed fuel card can provide an easy way to pay for gas while also limiting spending on a daily or weekly basis to keep everything on the table.

Go Oil offers a fuel card that is accepted at over 180K locations nationwide, provides driver spending limits, and creates detailed line-item spending reports to ensure that gas costs are always taken care of and expenses are kept entirely above-board.

Fleet maintenance and repairs

For every vehicle in your fleet, fleet services usually includes comprehensive maintenance and repairs. Keeping a fleet in top condition is what fleet services are designed to do best, and we get a lot of practice. A vehicle that is used to work does not need to be running all day.

The best way to get years of high-quality service from each vehicle is to stay on top of maintenance, and that's exactly what fleet services were originally designed to do.

Not every business that needs vehicles has the time or staff to keep a fleet tuned up and in top condition. A fleet service package can help you if your business doesn't have any mechanics. Fleet services professionals will do routine inspections and tuneups, and they can also design easy-to-follow maintenance routines that drivers can do between checkups to keep their vehicles in top condition.

Fleet Selection And Aquisition

Choosing the right vehicles for your fleet is not always easy. There are many makes and models available, with the manufacturer always having nice words to say about vehicles. But a fleet services expert knows how to select the exact vehicle for each business need.

They know the difference between a loud engine and hauling power. They can identify the kind of seat needed for all-day drivers versus special assignment drivers. You can tell which vehicles get better mileage in urban areas versus those that are used for rural purposes.
