Rent a vehicle

Requesting a rental from Go Oil is quick and easy using the Vehicle Rental Request form. This Fleet Services system offers:

  • NAU CAS Login - Use PeopleSoft to fill in the fields
  • Automated emails. First email confirms request has been submitted. Second email confirms Fleet Services has either 1) approved the request, 2) needs more information, or 3) has denied the request (very rare). The email is sent to the submitter, requester and driver.
  • You can include multiple vehicles and vehicle types as well as drivers.
  • Option to request Enterprise departure locations outside of NAU campus.
  • NAU as well as outside vendors can provide information about available classes of vehicles.
  • Calculator with built-in rates for estimates

Purchase

The Fleet Program Manager will assist departments in the purchase process. He or she will coordinate with Purchasing and Business Systems so that you can buy the vehicles as soon as possible. Please see PUR 211: Vehicles for more information on the procurement of vehicles.

Vehicle failures and emergencies

American Five Star, The Car Shop and RC 2 Automotive service fuel-powered vehicles. Electric trucks and carts are serviced at Naumann/Hobbs and Electric Vehicle Company.

ARS 41-803 (C) requires that costs for maintenance and operation of all state-owned vehicles be reported to the Arizona Department of Administration annually. Departments may maintain their vehicles outside of the program. However, departments must report the vehicle's maintenance and repair history to the Fleet Program Manager. As long as you can identify the vehicle and provide details about what services were provided, copies of bills for maintenance or repair should be sufficient.
